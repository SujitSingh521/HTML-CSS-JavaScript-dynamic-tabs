Fertility Solutions

Comprehensive Fertility Treatments

We offer a broad range of advanced treatments tailored to your unique circumstances:

ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)
ICSI is a treatment where a single sperm is injected directly into an egg to help fertilization, especially in male infertility cases.
IMSI
IMSI examines sperm at a microscopic level to ensure optimal selection, particularly for male infertility cases.
MicroChip Technology
MicroChip Technology uses a unique device to separate sperm based on their quality to increase the chances of successful fertilization.
EmbryoScope® Time-Lapse System
EmbryoScope® Time-Lapse System monitors embryo development with precision and helps select the best embryo for transfer.
